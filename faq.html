  • Q:My water is shut-off who do I contact?
  • A: In a situation where your water is shut-off customers need to make sure
  • the shut-off was performed to either one of the following reasons
  • 1. A Shut-off performed because of billing issues
  • 2. A Shut-off due to incompliance, failure to submit your Backflow Test Report
  • For billing issues refer to the number listed on your water bill
  • For Incompliance due to failure to submit a completed Backflow Test Report
  • contact the Cross-Connection mainline

  • Q:How do I get in contact with staff in the Cross-Connection Control Section?
  • A:Staff members can be reached by dialing the mainline and selecting their extension
