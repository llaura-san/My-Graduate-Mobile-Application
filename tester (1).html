How to Become A City Approved Backflow Tester

Accepted Backflow Testers Certification and Their official websites:

  • American Water Works Association (AWWA)

  • American Backflow Prevention Association (ABPA)

  • American Society of Sanitary Engineering (ASSE)

Certification Process


  • Please visit any of the following links above to find out how you can become a certified Backflow Tester
  • Certifications varies from State to State
  • *Some certifications require you to take Backflow Tester Training Courses at your local community college
  • *Here in San Diego County several colleges offer water programs Cuyamca College is one of them







City of San Diego Approved Backflow Tester List


  • Tester must be up-to-date with their certifications from any of the following associations: AWWA, ABPA, ASSE
  • Tester must have a valid driver's license
  • City of San Diego has an ongoing list of Backflow Testers who have been approved by the Cross-Connection Control Section
  • City of San Diego Approved Backflow Tester List
  • Tester must attend a one time required Backflow Tester Orientation held every January and July of each year
  • For more info or to sign-up for our Backflow Tester Orientation please contact lsan@sandiego.gov









Helpful Information

    Warning: Some backflow testers aren't really who they say they are
    Scammers are out there it is good to take caution when selecting a backflow tester
    You can verify a tester you can contact our office, you can also contact the agency certifying the tester to confirm the legitamcy of their license
